RM Capital employs a sophisticated two-tier approach to wealth growth, maximizing returns while minimizing risk exposure.

1

Primary Strategy: Option Selling

Short Strangle Strategy

We implement a carefully managed Short Strangle options strategy in your Demat account, selling both call and put options to collect premium income.

Consistent Monthly Returns

This strategy generates reliable returns of 3-5% monthly, providing a stable foundation for wealth growth.

Capital Preservation

Risk management is our priority, with strict position sizing and hedging techniques to protect your principal investment.

Risk Management Protocols

  • Strict position sizing (never over-leveraged)
  • Strategic strike price selection based on technical analysis
  • Continuous market monitoring
  • Hedging techniques during high volatility

Profit Reinvestment

Amplified Returns

How Our Dual Strategy Creates Compounding Growth

Step 1: Capital Deployment

Your capital is deployed in your own Demat account for option selling (Short Strangle strategy), remaining under your control at all times.

Step 2: Monthly Returns

The option strategy generates consistent 3-5% monthly returns from premium collection.

Step 3: Profit Allocation

Profits are split into three portions: Withdrawal for client use, Reinvestment into primary strategy, Investment into forex funded accounts.

Step 4: Forex Trading

We trade Gold (XAU/USD) using a portion of profits, creating a secondary income stream without risking your principal.

Step 5: Compounding Growth

As both strategies generate returns, total wealth grows at an accelerated rate through strategic reinvestment and compounding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our dual strategy approach.

Is my capital safe?

Your capital stays in your own Demat account at all times. We implement trading strategies in your account using our expertise, but you retain complete control over your funds. Our conservative risk management approach for the option selling strategy is designed to minimize potential drawdowns.

Can I withdraw my money anytime?

Yes, since the funds remain in your account, you have complete access at all times. We recommend a minimum commitment period of 6 months to see optimal results, but there are no lock-in periods or penalties for withdrawals.

Who trades on my behalf?

Our team of experienced options and forex traders, led by founder Shaik Rafi, executes the strategies. Each team member specializes in specific aspects of the dual approach, with extensive experience in Indian options markets and global forex trading.

What if the forex account fails funding?

While this is rare given our systematic approach, if a funded forex account fails to meet requirements, we simply allocate a new portion of profits to secure another account. There's no risk to your original capital since we only use a percentage of previously generated profits for forex trading.

What are the risks?

All investing carries some risk, but our strategies are designed to minimize exposure. The Short Strangle options strategy can face challenges during extreme market volatility, but our hedging protocols and position sizing minimize potential drawdowns. The forex component only risks profits already generated, never your original capital.

Do I need trading knowledge?

No, that's why you're hiring us. We handle all the technical aspects, market analysis, and trade execution. You'll receive regular reports explaining performance in clear terms, but the entire point of our service is to provide sophisticated trading without requiring you to become a trader yourself.

What is the minimum investment?

Our strategy works most effectively with a minimum capital of ₹10 lakhs, which allows for proper position sizing and risk management in the options component. For larger accounts above ₹50 lakhs, we offer customized approaches with additional diversification opportunities.

How do you get compensated?

We operate on a performance fee model, taking a percentage of profits generated. This aligns our interests with yours - we only succeed when you succeed. The exact fee structure is discussed during your initial consultation and depends on account size and strategy customization.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple. Schedule a consultation through our website or contact us directly. We'll discuss your financial goals, explain our approach in detail, and outline the next steps. Once you're ready to proceed, we'll guide you through the account setup process and begin implementing our strategy.
